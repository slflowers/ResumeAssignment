Shar-Narne Flowers

UX and Web Designer

Shar-Narne attended the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and graduated with a degree in Journalism and Mass Communications. Post graduation she briefly worked for several journalism and social media startups before joining Disney on Ice as a professional figure skater.

Currently, Shar-Narne works for a local Raleigh health non-profit as their website specialist and coaches figure skating at rinks within the RTP area.

Outside of work, you can find Shar-Narne playing with her cats, hiking and occasionally doing yoga.
